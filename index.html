#Motivation

My motivation is extremely simple expressed in one sentence.

Nevertheless, it constantly demands everything imaginable to be able to fulfill this inner aspiration.

"TOMORROW I WILL BE A BETTER VERSION OF YESTERDAY!"

I have deeply internalized this essence of my motivation and will only take a step back if I want to take a run-up.
